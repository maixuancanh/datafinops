# DataFinOps Demo Readiness Checklist

## Environment and Data

- [ ] Health reports sandbox, fixed clock, replay adapter, demo signer, and live-write disabled.
- [ ] Tenant, users, wallet references, pricing, usage, requirements, approvals, receipts, entitlement, and savings are synthetic.
- [ ] No production credential, funded wallet, private signing material, real purchase, or customer finance data appears.
- [ ] Seed/reset is deterministic and cannot address staging or production.

## Narrative

- [ ] Baseline, over-provisioning, import, optimization, hard-constraint change, approvals, unsigned summary, demo signing, confirmation, entitlement, and savings close appear in order.
- [ ] Every candidate displays independent hard-constraint status.
- [ ] The narration calls savings synthetic and distinguishes forecast from realized.
- [ ] Product boundaries state TxLINE-only, non-custodial, no betting, no automatic purchase, and no accounting advice.

## Capture and Render

- [ ] Playwright uses stable `data-testid` selectors and readiness states, not timing-only waits.
- [ ] Failed assertions retain trace/screenshots; success writes an execution report.
- [ ] Raw and final video are 1920x1080, 16:9, and 270 seconds or less.
- [ ] Network/program/mode/amount/proposal/entitlement evidence is readable and synthetic.
- [ ] Narration is clear, captions are synchronized, and audio is normalized.
- [ ] FFprobe output, video hash, config, script version, and report are archived.
